What are you doing? 你做緊乜嘢？ 你在做什麼？ What are you doing?

Nina Orange

Wiehan de Jager

Darshan Soni

英語

Level 1

I am singing.

我唱緊歌。

我在唱歌。

I am singing.

She is waving.

佢揮緊手。

她在揮手。

She is waving.

I am clapping.

我拍緊手。

我在拍手。

I am clapping.

She is stretching.

佢伸緊手。

她在伸手。

She is stretching.

He is calling.

佢叫緊。

他在喊叫。

He is calling.

I am answering.

我答緊。

我在回答。

I am answering.

She is listening.

佢聽緊。

她在聆聽。

She is listening.

What are you doing?

你做緊乜嘢？

你在做什麼？

What are you doing?
