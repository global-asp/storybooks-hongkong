Two 兩個 兩個 Two

Carole Bloch

Richard MacIntosh

Darshan Soni

英語

Level 1

Two little hands to hold.

兩隻手攞嚟揸住啲嘢。

兩隻手用來拿東西。

Two little hands to hold.

Two little feet to kick.

兩隻腳攞嚟踢吓波。

兩隻腳用來踢踢球。

Two little feet to kick.

Two little eyes to see.

兩隻眼攞嚟睇吓嘢。

兩隻眼睛用來看看。

Two little eyes to see.

Two little ears to hear.

兩隻小耳仔攞嚟聽吓嘢。

兩隻小耳朵用來聽聽。

Two little ears to hear.

And two loving arms to HUG!

仲有兩隻小手攞嚟抱吓你！

還有兩隻小手用來抱抱！

And two loving arms to HUG!

作者: Carole Bloch
插圖: Richard MacIntosh
